Cloud Security Checker

Answer these 10 questions about your cloud setup. You'll get a security score at the end.

1. Do you have MFA enabled on all cloud accounts?

2. Are your storage buckets (S3/GCS) set to private by default?

3. Do you rotate access keys and secrets regularly?

4. Do you have logging enabled (CloudTrail / GCP Audit Logs)?

5. Are your firewall/security group rules restricted to only needed ports?

6. Do you use IAM roles with least-privilege permissions?

7. Are your virtual machines patched and updated regularly?

8. Do you encrypt data at rest and in transit?

9. Do you have alerts set up for suspicious activity?

10. Do you have a documented incident response plan?
